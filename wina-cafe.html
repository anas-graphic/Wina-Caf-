Our Story

Born in the heart
of Slemani

"A cup of coffee shared with a friend is happiness tasted and time well spent."

— The spirit of Wina Café

Wina Café is Sulaymaniyah's cozy retreat — a space designed for unhurried moments, for catching-up conversations, for the quiet morning alone. We serve specialty coffee and handmade sweets in an atmosphere that genuinely feels like home.

Rooted in Kurdish warmth and hospitality, every detail at Wina is intentional. Come as a stranger, leave as a regular.
